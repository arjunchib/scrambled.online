# Potato Quiche

## Equipment
* 6in cast-iron skillet

## Ingredients
* 3 - 4 potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
* 4 eggs
* 4 slices of bacon, cut into 1in pieces
* shredded cheese
* milk, heavy whipping cream, or half-and-half
* 1/4 tsp smoked paprika
* dash cayenne powder
* dash chili powder
* dash garlic powder
* dash onion powder
* 1/4 tsp salt
* pepper
